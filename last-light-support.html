Last Light is an idle clicker about a self-replicating probe swarm consuming solar systems, one star at a time. Made by Had Games.

Common questions

Does my swarm work while the app is closed?
Yes — up to 12 hours banks automatically. Anything earned past the cap can be collected with an optional rewarded ad.

What carries over when I launch?
Blueprint Data and everything you've bought with it. Probes, matter, and the system itself are consumed. That's the point.

I bought Remove Ads on a new device.
Purchases restore automatically on first launch. If the banner persists, force-quit the app and reopen it once.

My progress is gone.
Progress is stored on-device and is removed if the app is deleted. Email us and we'll do what we can.
